Dice Duel

Instructions:

Players should bet a number on themselves on whether they're going to win before Rolling the Dice.

Once each player has put their bets in they should Roll the dice.

Whoever has the higher dice roll number recives how much they betted and who ever loses subtracts what they betted from their points.

Repeat consecutively for 5 rounds and whoever has the most points at the end wins the game!
